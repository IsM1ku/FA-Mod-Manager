>>Event Overview<<
Make a beeline through the Enforcer's territory. Stop for nothing.

Player races against the clock and numerous Enforcer ambushers. 
The Enforcer rival ambushes at the midpoint of the track.

Primary Objective:
-Reach the end of the Enforcer's territory in 3 minutes or less.

Secondary Objectives:
-Finish in 2 minutes or less.
-Kill the Enforcer Rival.
-Kill 6 Enforcers.

Event play by play:
-Player chased by 2 enforcers at start.
-Must crash through or avoid roadblock under overpass
-Crash through roadblock on highway
-Several enforcers pursue off highway.
-Rival jumps off of overpass down onto road at end of highway.
-Two head on enforcers attack shortly after rival ambush.
-Player must then navigate a minefield in the marketplace alleys
-Finally the player must crash a roadblock at the end of the race.
*/
